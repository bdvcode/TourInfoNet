The citizens of the Schengen Agreement, as well as the UK, Switzerland, Japan, there is an opportunity to issue short-term tourist visa (up to 72 hours) with the following tour operators Kaliningrad: Annie tel. +7 (4012) 35-52-34
Baltma bodies. +7 (4012) 93-19-31
Briesen bodies. +7 (4012) 77-34-32
Nocturne tel. +7 (4012) 53-80-71
Sven Tour bodies. +7 (4012) 95-43-99
Universalstroy bodies. +7 (4012) 66-90-50

To obtain a visa you must provide the following information in any of the above companies not later than 72 hours prior to arrival in the Kaliningrad region:

  1. Scanned copy of passport
  2. Work and home address and telephone number;
  3. Arrival date and border crossing:
  • Bagrationovsk
  • Mamonowo - Airport Hrabrovo (in the case of arriving at the airport, you should have a color photo of 3 x 4 cm)
  • the Baltic Port you have chosen a travel agency can book your hotel (prerequisite), will issue health insurance and other necessary documents, as well as the representative of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs will take place at the border crossing.
